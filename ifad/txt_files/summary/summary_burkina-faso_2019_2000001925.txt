The goal of PAFA-4R is to contribute to reducing poverty and stimulating economic growth in the Southwest, Hauts-Bassins, Cascades and Boucle du Mouhoun regions.
The development objective of PAFA-4R replicates that of PAPFA, namely: sustainably improve food security and incomes of farmers engaged in production and value addition in the value chains supported by the project.
