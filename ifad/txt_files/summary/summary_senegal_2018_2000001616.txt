The goal of PADAER-II is to contribute to reducing poverty for women, young people and men living in the programme area by integrating them with profitable, diversified value chains that are resilient to climate change. The development objective is to sustainably improve the food security and incomes of smallholder crop and livestock farmers and to create permanent, well-paying jobs for rural people, particularly women and young people.
